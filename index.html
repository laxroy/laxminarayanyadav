Services

Modern web & municipal IT services.

From responsive websites to office automation and verified IT supply — I help organizations run efficiently and securely.

Website Creation

Portfolio & business sites with responsive UI, accessibility, SEO (schema), and analytics. Tech: HTML/CSS/JS, React/Next.js, WordPress when needed, Cloudflare/GitHub Pages hosting.
